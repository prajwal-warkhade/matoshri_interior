MATOSHRI INTERIOR

This is a highly unusual course at the cutting edge of one of Ahmednagar's fastest growing areas of design practice. This also tends with an every increasing range of employment opportunities in the field.

Institute for studies in Design and Technology (ISDT) is the only institution in the district which is affiliated to SNDT Women's University Mumbai (NAAC five star Accreditation) ISDT is having own and beautiful building at the heart of city. The Institute believes in offering programmes of study in the design discipline. This encourages the students to attain higher standards of education.

The teaching team includes dedicated and full time Architects, Interior Designers, Engineers, with additional industry guest speakers, practicing Designers and Professionals. This help students to get the Professional knowledge and Training. Special stress also given towards the way, procedure and proper execution of site with proper presentation of drawings.

You will work in specialist design and drawing studios with access to industry standard design softwares like AutoCAD, 3D Studio Max design, Revit etc. We are proud to mention that we are taking the optimum educational efforts and train the students for bright future. This diploma could lead you to a better job, better pay, higher promotion or your own business.
